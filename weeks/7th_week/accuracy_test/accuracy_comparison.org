*** Accuracy Comparison of Fur and Seqwin

I checked the accuracy difference between two both with different lenghts of the markers
and length of the sequence: 100000:

#+begin_src bash
  bash driver.sh 100000 seqwin
  bash driver.sh 100000 fur
  bash summarize.sh seqwin
  bash summarize.sh fur
#+end_src


accuracy of fur with different marker lenghts:

|           200 | 0.957186 |  0.00679441 |
|           400 | 0.966305 |  0.00289561 |
|           800 | 0.983795 |   0.0021336 |
|          1600 |  0.99059 | 0.000934393 |
|          3200 | 0.995114 | 0.000347968 |
|          7500 | 0.997541 | 0.000270375 |
