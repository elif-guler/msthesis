*** Speed Comparison Between Fur and Seqwin

I ran both tools with 1 thread option.

I used stan to generate simulated targets and neighbors datasets. There are four results with four
different size of neighbors and targets.
- n 10 t 10 -l 5000000
- n 20 t 20 -l 5000000
- n 50 t 50 -l 5000000    
- n 100 t 100 -l 5000000 

I also checked their speed by changing the lenght of the sequence
- l 10000
- l 100000
- l 200000
- l 500000    
- l 1000000
- l 10000000
