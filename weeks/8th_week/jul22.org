*** Generating the graph shown in Seqwin paper
in the graph, fraction of nontarget hits were 1 for almost all the
markers. we applied a lower evalue to the blast to align the results
with how fur generates the results. in that case, most of the markers
had fraction of nontarget hits lower than 0.25. for the one that had 0.8,
i checked manually:

#+begin_src bash
awk -F',' 'NR==1{for(i=1;i<=NF;i++) if($i=="fraction_nontarget_hit") col=i; print; next} $col>0.75' cons_div_results.csv
getSeq "RJLJ01001116.1_1556" markers.fasta > outlier.fasta

for f in neighbors/*.fasta; do
    echo "===== $f ====="
    blastn \
      -task blastn \
      -evalue 1e-5 \
      -query outlier.fasta \
      -subject "$f" \
      -outfmt "6"
done
#+end_src
